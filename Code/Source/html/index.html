What is Asteroids?
There have been many versions of the classic computer game "Asteroids". A lot of these versions have been implemented with the idea of a single player. This version of Asteroids, is completely different to the rest of them. This one is multiplayer.

Tell me a bit more about Asteroids
You control a spaceship, and you fly around the game shooting asteroids and other players.

What are the objectives?

The main objective is to win the game, this is achieved by getting the most points within the game, points are awarded for:

  • 30 points for a big asteroid
  • 40 points for a small asteroid
  • 100 points for another ship

How do I control my ship?

You control your ship by using the keyboard:

  • Accelerate forward: Up Arrow
  • Turn left: left arrow
  • Turn right: right arrow
  • Shoot: Space-bar

You can turn your sound on or off by hitting the "s" button on your keyboard

Note: There is no deceleration, to decelerate turn your ship through 180 degrees and accelerate (ie accelerate the opposite direction)
